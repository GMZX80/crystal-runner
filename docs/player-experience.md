# Player Experience

## Intended Feel

Crystal Runner should feel quick, readable, and retryable. The player should understand the goal from the first visible cannon/crest pattern rather than from a long tutorial.

## Emotional Beat

- First 10 seconds: curiosity and control discovery.
- First crest: clear reward.
- First dodge: competence.
- First failure: fair cause and instant retry.
- Later run: rising pressure as speed increases.

## Feedback Plan

- Crest: gold object disappears, score increases, short high tone.
- Lane move: short tone.
- Obstacle collision: low tone, game-over screen, final score.
- Scoring: HUD updates continuously.

## Current Gaps

- No reduced-motion toggle yet.
- No persistent settings menu yet.
- Cannon hazards and crests now have distinct sprites, but still need real-player accessibility feedback.
